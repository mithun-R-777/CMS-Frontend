About ContentSphere

ContentSphere is a dynamic and interactive content management system designed to empower users to create, share, and engage with content effortlessly. Our platform offers a variety of features to enhance user experience and foster community interaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a seamless and intuitive platform for content creators to express their creativity and connect with their audience. We strive to make content creation and sharing as easy and enjoyable as possible.

Project Information

This project is an internship project done for Edubridge.

TechStack Used

Backend: Spring Boot, Hibernate, Java
Frontend: Angular, Bootstrap
